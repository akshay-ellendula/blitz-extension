Download

Download the BLITZ zip file from this page and unzip it to a folder on your computer.

2

3

Done!

FAQ

Questions Answered

Is BLITZ free? +
Yes, completely free. Download and install it manually — no payment, no account required.
Does it work on every website? +
Yes. BLITZ scans any page for buttons matching its built-in keyword list (e.g. "Buy Now", "Join Queue", "Book"). You can also add custom keywords for site-specific buttons.
Will it get my account banned? +
BLITZ is designed to behave like a fast human user — it only clicks buttons that are visible and enabled. You can adjust the scan speed to stay within normal usage patterns on stricter platforms.
Does BLITZ collect my personal data or login details? +
Never. BLITZ runs entirely in your browser and on your device. It has no servers, sends no data anywhere, and cannot see your passwords or payment details.
Why do I need to install it manually? +
Manual "unpacked" installation means you get the extension directly — no app store review delays. Developer Mode is a built-in Chrome feature for exactly this purpose and is perfectly safe.
